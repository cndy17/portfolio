Analysis of how the US Electoral College shapes Presidential outcomes using spatial data and official electoral records. Examines which states have outsized influence relative to population and models alternative allocation systems.

## Key questions

Which states have the highest electoral votes per capita? How would outcomes change under proportional allocation or a national popular vote?

## Methods

Official electoral allocations cross-referenced with Census population data. Spatial visualization using Python GIS libraries. Historical election results overlaid to show which states actually decided each election.
