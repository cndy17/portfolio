Predicting IMDb ratings using Python and Pandas. Full ML workflow: data cleaning, feature engineering, model selection, cross-validation, and evaluation across multiple regression approaches.

## Key insight

Vote count is the strongest predictor of rating stability. After controlling for it, genre and decade of release are the next most predictive features.
